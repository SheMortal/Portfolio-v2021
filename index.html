Who is Sinothando?

I am a Cape Town based aspiring web developer with a passion for both front & back end development. In February 2020, I enrolled in a coding course at LifeChoices Academy. Since then I have been exposed in a field full of diversity of thought, perspective and culture.

Being a women in the world of 1s and 0s gave a rise to being a little uncomfortable with my skills, that I later on took as a sign of learning. Where you have the chance to be uncomfortable, you learn new things.

I aspire toward a path that will allow me to channel my creativity and innovation. My undertaking is to take all the knowledge and skills I have acquired and not only incite, but cross all platforms and give back to young women where I come from.
